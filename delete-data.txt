GigFlux – Delete Your Data
==========================

This page explains how to request deletion of your data in the GigFlux app.


What data GigFlux stores about you
-----------------------------------
  - Your self-chosen nickname within a group
  - Your band ratings (values 0–3 per band)
  - A randomly generated session token (stored in your browser)
  - Your join timestamp

No email address, real name, or password is ever required or stored.


Option 1: Delete your data from within the app
-----------------------------------------------
You can delete your own account at any time directly in the app:

  1. Open the app and navigate to your group
  2. Go to Settings (within the group view)
  3. Select "Leave / Delete account"

This removes your nickname and all your ratings from the group immediately.


Option 2: Contact the administrator
-------------------------------------
If you cannot access the app or need your data deleted from all groups,
send a request to:

  contact@dino-code.com

Please include the group Name, nickmane and invite link so we can identify your data.
Requests are processed within 30 days.


What gets deleted
------------------
  - Deleted immediately: your nickname, ratings, and session token
  - Retained briefly: IP addresses in standard server logs
    (automatically overwritten, no permanent logging)
